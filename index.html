✨ A poem for ✨

Rhian

the girl who never sleeps... until she does

She dances when the midnight strikes,
She's everyone's favourite wherever she likes,
The music calls and she answers first,
For fun and laughter she has an endless thirst.
The life of the party, the queen of the night,
She sparkles and shimmers in every light,
The last to leave, the first to say
"One more dance, and then we'll see..."
But when the morning sneaks back in,
And sunlight taps upon the chin,
She pulls the duvet, soft and deep,
And gives the world the gift of sleep.
Party hard and sleep till two,
There's nobody quite like you,
A shooting star by night, a sleepy queen by day —
Rhian, you're wonderful in every single way. 💕
